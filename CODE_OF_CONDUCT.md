# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: keep collaboration respectful, practical, and safe for public open-source work.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss ideas and tradeoffs clearly.
- Assume documentation may be read by people with different operational constraints.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Protect private information and operational safety.
- Prefer small, reviewable changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Publishing private data or operational secrets.
- Encouraging unsafe production access or unapproved paid tool use.
- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Deliberately adding misleading or unsafe operational guidance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate public-safety requirements or make the repository unsafe to use.
